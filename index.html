Flight of the Monarchs

Pause

Every year, millions of eastern Monarch butterflies make the massive trek from their overwintering grounds at the Oyamel Fir Forest in central Mexico, to one of a variety of breeding grounds in the US and Canada, and back again.

In this visual, I use iNaturalist sighting data to animate their remarkable migration.

Each point on the map represents a Monarch sighting.
